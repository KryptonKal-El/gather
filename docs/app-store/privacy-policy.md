# Gather — Privacy Policy

*Last updated: March 3, 2026*

## Introduction

Gather ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you sign in with Apple, we receive:
- **Email address** — Used to create your account. You may choose to use Apple's private relay email.
- **Name** — Your first and last name, used to personalize your experience. Apple only provides this on your first sign-in.

### Shopping List Data
- Items you add to your shopping lists (names, quantities, categories)
- Lists you create and their settings (name, color, shared status)
- Checked/unchecked status of items

### Product Search Data
- Photos you submit for AI product identification are processed in real-time and are **not stored** after identification is complete
- Search queries for product lookup

### Usage Data
- We collect minimal technical data required for the app to function (e.g., authentication tokens, session data)
- We do **not** collect analytics, crash reports, device identifiers, or location data

## How We Use Your Information

- **Account creation and authentication** — To sign you in and maintain your session
- **Shopping list functionality** — To store, sync, and share your lists across devices
- **AI features** — To process product images and provide search results
- **Shared lists** — To enable real-time collaboration with people you choose to share with

## Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted on AWS infrastructure). Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.

## Data Sharing

We do **not**:
- Sell your personal data to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your data for targeted advertising
- Track you across other apps or websites

We share data **only** in these cases:
- **With people you explicitly share lists with** — They can see list items and your display name
- **With our infrastructure providers** (Supabase/AWS) — Solely for hosting and data storage purposes

## Third-Party Services

- **Supabase** — Authentication and database hosting ([Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy))
- **Apple Sign-In** — Authentication ([Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/))
- **Open Food Facts** — Product data lookup (open-source, no personal data sent)
- **SerpAPI** — Fallback product image search (search queries only, no personal data)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your data — All your lists and account info are visible in the app
- **Delete** your data — Contact us at the support URL below to request account deletion
- **Port** your data — Your shopping lists can be accessed from any device

## Children's Privacy

Gather does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is rated 4+ but is designed for general audiences.

## Data Retention

- Your account and list data are retained as long as your account is active
- Deleted lists are permanently removed from our servers
- Product search images are processed in real-time and not retained

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through the app.

## Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or data deletion requests:
- Website: https://gatherapp.vercel.app
- In-app: Settings > Support
