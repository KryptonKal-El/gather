## Description (4000 chars max)

ShoppingListAI is your intelligent grocery shopping companion that makes meal planning and shopping effortless.

**Smart Shopping Lists**
Create and organize shopping lists with ease. Add items quickly, check them off as you shop, and keep everything organized by category. Your lists sync across all your devices instantly.

**AI-Powered Features**
• Snap a photo of any product and our AI identifies it instantly — no typing needed
• Get smart recipe suggestions based on items in your list
• AI helps you discover new products with image-based search

**Share & Collaborate**
Share your shopping lists with family members, roommates, or anyone. Everyone sees updates in real-time, so you never buy duplicates again.

**Works Everywhere**
Start your list on your iPhone, check it on your iPad, or access it from any web browser. Your data syncs seamlessly across all platforms.

**Privacy First**
Sign in securely with Apple. Your data is yours — we don't sell it, track you, or show ads. Ever.

**Key Features:**
• Create unlimited shopping lists
• AI product image search — snap to add
• Real-time shared lists with family & friends
• Smart recipe suggestions
• Beautiful dark mode support
• Works offline — sync when you're back online
• Organize items with custom categories and colors
• Secure Apple Sign-In
