## Description (4000 chars max)

Gather Lists is your intelligent grocery shopping companion that makes meal planning and shopping effortless.

**Smart Shopping Lists**
Create and organize shopping lists with ease. Add items quickly, check them off as you shop, and keep everything organized by category. Your lists sync across all your devices instantly.

**Product Image Search**
• Search for any product by name and instantly browse product images
• Add items to your list with a tap — no manual entry needed
• Powered by a rich product database covering millions of grocery items

**Share & Collaborate**
Share your shopping lists with family members, roommates, or anyone. Everyone sees updates in real-time, so you never buy duplicates again.

**Recipes & Meal Planning**
Browse and save recipes, plan meals, and add ingredients straight to your list. Discover new dishes and build your shopping list from your favorite recipes in one tap.

**Works Everywhere**
Start your list on your iPhone, check it on your iPad, or access it from the Gather Lists web app at gatherlists.com. Your data syncs seamlessly across all platforms.

**Privacy First**
Sign in securely with Apple. Your data is yours — we don't sell it, track you, or show ads. Ever.

**Key Features:**
• Create unlimited shopping lists
• Product image search — find items instantly by name
• Real-time shared lists with family & friends
• Recipes & meal planning — save recipes and add ingredients to your list
• Beautiful dark mode support
• Works offline — sync when you're back online
• Organize items with custom categories and colors
• Secure Apple Sign-In
